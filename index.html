About

I love to bring visions into reality. My urge to create beauty and insistent desire to build things in this world have informed my experience as an entrepreneur, artist, graphic designer and ultimately web developer.

My work experience at non-profits and startups has taught me how to work both collaboratively and independently to bring ideas to fruition. I enjoy the entire creative process- from ideation, to design, to creative problem solving while building a project. I also especially enjoy the satisfaction of shipping a product and presenting a job well done.

I am constantly seeking to learn more about the world and how it works, and am equally eager about building towards a better world.

I hold a B.S. in Conservation and Resource Studies from UC Berkeley, an M.B.A. from Quantic School of Business and Technology, and Fullstack Web Development Certificate from Le Wagon Coding Bootcamp.

RobinGood

RobinGood was a capstone project I built with a team at Le Wagon Coding Bootcamp. Users sign up to receive RobinGood QR codes which they can place on their items. If they lose their item, anyone can scan the QR code to securly communicate to the owner that they have found their item and left it at a safe location. The owner is then alerted by RobinGood about their item's whereabouts

Once the user signs up for an account, they receive three QR codes

They then assign the QR codes to the items they plan to attach them to

If the item is lost, anyone can scan the QR code with a mobile device. They will follow the process below to input data about the item

Back on the owners side, they will receive an email and notification that their item has been found. They must then pass through a paywall asking them to make a donation to a local cause to view the item's location

Then they can go retrieve their item!
